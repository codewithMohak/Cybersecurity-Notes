## The Burp proxy is a fundamental tool and important within the **BURPSUITE**.

### Key Point to understand the working of **PROXY**
### **Intercepting Request**: 
  * When request are made through Burp Proxy they are intercept and hold back before reaching to the targeted server.
  * The request appears in **PROXY TAB** , allowing for further actions such as *forwarding*, *dropping*, *editing*, or *sending*.
### **Taking Control**:
*  The ability to intercept requests empowers takes the complete control over the web traffics.
### **Capturing and Logging**:
- **BURPSUITE** captures the requests logs made through the proxy, by default even if the Interception is **OFF**.
### **WebSocket Support**:
- **BURPSUITE** also captures and log of WebSocket communication.
### **Logs and History**:
- Captured requests are shown in the HTTP History and WebSockets History tabs. 
- You can review them later and send them to other Burp tools when needed.
### **Proxy -Specific**:
 - You can access proxy-specific options by clicking the **Proxy settings** button. 
 - These options let you control how the Proxy works and behaves. 
 - Learn these settings properly to use Burp Proxy more effectively.
